Welcome to my website.


Research Interests :
Intelligent Systems, Embedded Systems, Healthcare systems, Wireless Technologies, Cyber Physical Systems, IoT (Internet of Things).

I have worked as a Technical Associate at Robert Bosch Centre for Cyber Physical Systems (RBCCPS) at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore. Presently I am working on numerous projects in the fields of Healthcare/ Health informatics, Assistive technology, Resource Monitoring, IoT, CPS, etc. I previously worked in Embedded Systems Lab at Department of Electronic Systems Engineering ( DESE, formerly Centre For Electronics Design And Technology or CEDT) on the Project "A Cyber Physical System for Remote Health Monitoring of Elderly" sponsored by DeitY (Formerly DIT) Govt. of INDIA.

I have completed my bachelors in Electronics and Communication from ICFAI University in 2011. I have a passion for Embedded systems, Intelligent Systems and a strong believer of IoT. Moreover, I am intrigued by numerous technologies that helps us build a better future, such as HCI (Human Computer Interaction), AI (Artificial Intelligence ), ML (Machine Learning), Robotics, Virtual Reality and so on.

Apart from work; I love working on various personal projects and love Learning and contributing to upcoming technologies. My project are inspired by the challenges we face in our day to day activities, problems that are both challenging and at the same time can be solved using simple technology based solutions. Also, I help fellow engineers and friends as a part of numerous communities like Google+, Bangalore Makespace and open source community [BMOSC-Labs], Element14 Community, Hackster.io Community and Blog about my own projects.
